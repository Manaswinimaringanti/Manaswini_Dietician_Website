Background

About Me

I am a motivated and detail-oriented dietician and M.Sc. Clinical Nutrition graduate with hands-on hospital internship experience at Jaipur National University Hospital. My clinical training spans medical nutrition therapy, therapeutic diet planning, and patient counselling across a wide spectrum of conditions — from renal and hepatic disease to critical care and post-surgical recovery.

I bring a rigorous, evidence-based approach to every patient interaction, combining strong biochemical analysis skills with a genuine commitment to behaviour change and patient empowerment. Whether designing enteral feeding protocols in the ICU or counselling outpatients on chronic disease management, I prioritise personalised, goal-oriented nutrition care.

Academically, I have contributed a peer-reviewed book chapter on food safety and foodborne illness, presented at national and international conferences, and completed a Master's research thesis developing a calcium-enriched functional food product. I am ready to bring this blend of clinical rigour, research competence, and patient-centred practice to a hospital, clinic, or wellness setting.
